# TravelWell Voice



## Vision



TravelWell Voice is a voice-first travel companion that helps travelers coordinate trips through one natural conversation.



Unlike traditional travel apps that require switching between airline, hotel, transportation, dining, and activity applications, TravelWell Voice orchestrates the travel experience using conversational AI.



---



## Problem



Modern travel is fragmented.



Travelers constantly switch between:

- airline apps

- hotel apps

- rideshare

- restaurants

- activities

- email confirmations



When disruptions occur, travelers manually rebuild their plans.



---



## Solution



TravelWell Voice combines:



- Vocal Bridge

- Sabre APIs

- AI orchestration



to provide one conversational interface that understands the trip, identifies disruptions, and recommends the next best actions.

The Problem

Existing travel engines manage transactions, not traveler health.

Travel data is heavily fragmented across airlines, hotels, transport networks, and email folders. The human traveler is left to manually coordinate dietary needs, fitness schedules, and stress management reactively when delays occur.

The Core Insight

Optimize the traveler, not just the itinerary.

Sabre Context

  • Live trip and booking information
  • Flight status and timing
  • Verified travel parameters

Traveler Memory

  • Preferences and habits
  • Connected memberships
  • Wellness routines
  • Loyalty and brand choices

Voice-to-Action Workflow

Action Gated by Traveler Approval

Vocal Bridge stream routes voice commands to FastAPI. Gemini on Vertex AI processes the user intent, queries the Sabre MCP Skills server, and updates the itinerary. No reservation is finalized without the traveler's voice approval.
